School Canteen

Our School was founded in 1 June 1886 (Including the establish of Canteen)

The College Canteen is the essential part of the colleges and a popular place both with students and staff. It is located on the ground floor and easily accessible to all. The canteen is well equipped with furniture, hall and a cabin to cater to the staff of the college. There is a variety of food items served in a clean, fresh condition and at reasonable rates. It is perhaps the busiest place in the college where every student is expected to pay at least one or may be two visits a day for quick snacks, cold drinks, tea, some gossip or a discussion on courses taught, youth festival, talent show etc.
